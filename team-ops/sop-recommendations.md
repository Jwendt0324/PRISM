## Process Gaps — New SOPs Needed

### 1. Client Onboarding SOP
- **Why:** [Your Ops Admin] currently handles ad hoc. No standardized intake email template, no checklist verified.
- **What it should cover:** Intake email template, Basecamp project setup, RACI assignment, client welcome message, prerequisite collection checklist
- **Priority:** High — directly impacts Sprint Day 0-2

### 2. Financial Reporting SOP
- **Why:** [Your Mentor/Advisor] has been pushing for formal P&L. No monthly financial reporting in place. Revenue distribution is informal.
- **What it should cover:** Monthly P&L template, revenue tracking, expense categorization, distribution calculation, quarterly report to equity holders
- **Priority:** High — equity agreement requires quarterly financial summaries upon request

### 3. AI Apprentice Delivery SOP
- **Why:** Apprentices need standardized work assignments, quality gates, and completion criteria. Currently managed informally.
- **What it should cover:** Task assignment from Sprint labor pool, quality checklist per task type, mentor review process, certification completion criteria
- **Priority:** Medium — becomes critical as apprentice cohort grows

### 4. Case Study Production SOP
- **Why:** "If it's not published, it's not done" — but no SOP exists for how to produce a case study from a completed Sprint.
- **What it should cover:** Case study template, data collection during Sprint (before/after metrics), client approval process, publication checklist
- **Priority:** High — required for every Sprint

### 5. Ad Account Management SOP
- **Why:** Multiple Google Ads getting disapproved. No one assigned to monitor.
- **What it should cover:** Weekly ad account health check, policy compliance review before submission, escalation for disapprovals
- **Priority:** Medium — impacts Dollar-a-Day campaigns

### 6. Tool Access Management SOP
- **Why:** Jumper Media admin was unknown (nobody could identify who set it up). Shared credentials create security risk.
- **What it should cover:** Master access registry (who has access to what), onboarding/offboarding checklist, shared credential rotation schedule, admin contact for each tool
- **Priority:** Medium — prevents "who set this up?" situations

### 7. Content Calendar / Publishing Cadence SOP
- **Why:** Target is 3-5 shorts/week + 1 long-form/week per channel. No SOP for managing this cadence.
- **What it should cover:** Content calendar template, assignment workflow, publishing checklist per platform, repurposing pipeline
- **Priority:** Medium — [Your Content Specialist] needs this to manage his workload

### 8. Sprint Retrospective SOP
- **Why:** No retro happens after Sprint delivery. Lessons learned aren't captured.
- **What it should cover:** Post-Sprint meeting template (30 min), what went well/what didn't/what to change, SOP update triggers, client satisfaction capture
- **Priority:** Medium — compounds operational improvement over time
