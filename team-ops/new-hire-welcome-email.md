# [New Hire Name]'s Welcome Email

**Ready to send. Copy everything below the line.**

---

**Subject:** Welcome to [Your Agency] — Here's What You Need to Know

[New Hire Name],

Welcome to [Your Agency Name]. I'm glad you're here and I'm going to be straight with you about what this looks like.

You're joining at the ground floor of something real. We help founder-led businesses build influence — Google Knowledge Panels, personal brand sites, authority content, the whole stack. Our clients pay $6,000 for a 30-day Sprint and we deliver results that change how they show up online. We also run the AI Apprentice Program where we train the next generation of digital marketers using the Content Factory methodology.

**Your role:** Content Specialist. You're responsible for article writing, content repurposing, video editing, and design work. Everything you produce needs to meet the standard — no AI-generated language, real journalism, real value.

**What success looks like in your first 30 days:**
- Complete 5 articles that pass QA ([Your Mentor/Advisor] and [Your Ops Partner] will review — their feedback is law)
- Produce 3 short-form videos with subtitles, branding, and hooks
- Complete all tool onboarding and be fully operational in Basecamp
- Attend every weekly meeting and submit your EOD report daily

**Your first week:**
- **Day 1:** Read these SOPs — Knowledge Panel Sprint, Article Writing from Transcripts, Article QA & Compliance. These are in the PRISM at ~/Documents/Claude/PRISM/sops/client-work/
- **Day 2:** Shadow me on [Client — Local Retail Business] work. Write your first article from a transcript.
- **Day 3:** Get feedback, revise. No shortcuts.
- **Day 4:** Attend AI Apprentice Office Hours (Thursday, 2pm PST on Zoom)
- **Day 5:** Submit your first MAA report (Metrics, Analysis, Action)

**Tools you should have access to:**
- Basecamp (project work — this is where everything happens)
- Google Workspace ([team-email@your-agency.com])
- Descript (video/audio editing — shared account via [Your Ops Admin])
- WP Admin for [your-agency-domain.com] and [partner-platform.com]
- YouTube channels
- Shared Google Drive
- Facebook group ([your-facebook-group-url])

If you're missing access to anything, email [Your Ops Admin] ([ops-admin@partner-platform.com]). He handles all tool access.

**How we communicate:**
- **Basecamp** for all project work. Not email. If it's about a client or a task, it goes in Basecamp.
- **Email** for direct 1:1 stuff between us.
- **EOD reports** in Basecamp every day — what you did, what's next, what's blocked.
- **MAA reports** every Friday — Metrics, Analysis, Action.

**Who you'll work with:**
- **Me ([Your Name]):** Your direct manager. Daily check-ins. I assign your work.
- **[Your Mentor/Advisor]:** Our strategic advisor. He will review your work. When he gives feedback, follow it exactly. He's been doing this for decades.
- **[Your Ops Partner] [Last Name]:** Operations. He'll help you with processes and quality standards.
- **[Your Ops Admin] [Last Name]:** Admin and ops. Tool access, onboarding, anything operational.

**One thing to know:** [Your Mentor/Advisor] and [Your Ops Partner] will hold you to a high standard. They flagged AI language in your first article — that can't happen again. Write like a journalist telling a real story. Use real examples, real quotes, real insights. If you're using AI to help draft, you need to rewrite it until it sounds like a human wrote it from experience.

I brought you on because I trust you. Now prove it with the work. Show up every day, do the reps, and you'll grow with this company.

Let's go.

Love Always,

[Your Name]
Founder | [Your Agency Name]
