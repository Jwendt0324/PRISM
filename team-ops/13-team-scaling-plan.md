# 13 — Team Scaling Plan

**How [[memory-bank/01-hri-overview|[Your Agency]]] grows from 5 people to 10 — and when to pull each trigger.**

---

## Current State

**Team size:** 5 ([Your Name], [Your Mentor/Advisor], [Your Ops Partner], [Your Ops Admin], [Your Content Specialist])
**Current capacity:** ~2-3 active Sprints at a time
**Bottleneck:** [Your Name] is CEO, lead salesperson, AND project manager on every Sprint
**Target:** 12 Sprints in 90 days (4/month sustained)
**Revenue projection:** $250k Year 1 (tuition + PAL + DFY combined)

---

## Next 3 Hires (In Order of Priority)

### Hire #1: Delivery PM / Project Manager

**Trigger:** 4+ concurrent Sprints

**Why this is first:** [Your Name] is currently PM on every Sprint. That means every client question, every blocker, every status update flows through him. This bottlenecks both sales (he can't prospect while managing delivery) and delivery (clients wait for his attention). Removing [Your Name] from day-to-day Sprint management is the single highest-leverage hire we can make.

**Role:**
- Manage Sprint execution from Day 3 through Day 30
- Own client communication during active delivery
- Identify and resolve blockers before they become escalations
- Run the red/amber/green status updates
- Ensure QA checkpoints are hit on schedule

**Compensation:** 10% per Sprint ($600) + QA bonus eligibility

**Where to find them:** AI Apprentice program graduates who've completed multiple Sprints and proven they understand the Content Factory methodology. They already know the system — no ramp time.

**Success metric:** [Your Name] spends zero hours on active Sprint management within 30 days of hire.

---

### Hire #2: Content Writer / Editor

**Trigger:** 6+ concurrent Sprints

**Why this is second:** Article production is the capacity constraint after PM work is delegated. [Your Content Specialist] can handle 2-3 Sprints of content, but at 6 Sprints he'll be writing 30-60 articles simultaneously. Quality drops when volume exceeds capacity. We need a second writer before that happens.

**Role:**
- Write authority articles from interview transcripts
- SEO editing and optimization
- Follow the Article Writing from Transcripts SOP
- Pass [Your Mentor/Advisor]'s quality bar (no AI language, journalist standard)

**Compensation:** Per-article rate from the delivery labor pool

**Where to find them:** Apprentice pipeline first (they already know the methodology), then referrals from the [Methodology Partner] network. Must complete a qualifying task (repurpose a Marketing Mechanic video) reviewed by [Your Mentor/Advisor]/[Your Ops Partner] before starting paid work.

**Success metric:** Article output doubles without quality scores dropping.

---

### Hire #3: Sales / BD Representative

**Trigger:** 8+ Sprints/month AND [Your Name] is fully PM-free AND pipeline exceeds [Your Name]'s closing capacity

**Why this is third:** Sales is [Your Name]'s highest-value activity. He shouldn't delegate it until (a) delivery runs without him and (b) there are more qualified leads than he can personally close. Hiring BD too early means paying someone to generate leads [Your Name] doesn't have time to work.

**Role:**
- Outbound prospecting using the GTM playbook
- Qualification calls (BANT framework)
- Pipeline management and CRM hygiene
- Hand off qualified opportunities to [Your Name] for closing

**Compensation:** 15% sales commission ($900/Sprint)

**Where to find them:** Apprentice graduates who've shown sales aptitude, or referrals from the [Methodology Partner] network. Must understand the Knowledge Panel Sprint offer cold before starting outreach.

**Success metric:** Pipeline grows 3x within 60 days of hire. [Your Name]'s close rate stays above 30%.

| 6 | $36k | Hire Content Writer. Article capacity doubles. |
| 8 | $48k | Hire BD Rep. Pipeline generation delegated. |

## Team Structure at 10 People

```
[Your Name] — CEO / Sales
├── [Your Mentor/Advisor] — Advisor / Quality Lead
├── [Your Ops Partner] — Operations
├── [Your Ops Admin] — Admin / Finance
├── [Your Content Specialist] — Content Lead
│   ├── Content Writer #1
│   └── Content Writer #2
├── Delivery PM #1
├── Delivery PM #2
└── BD Rep
```

**Reporting lines:**
- Content Writers report to [Your Content Specialist], who reports to [Your Name]
- Delivery PMs report to [Your Name] (eventually to a Head of Delivery if we hit 15+ Sprints)
- BD Rep reports to [Your Name]
- [Your Ops Admin] handles admin/finance, coordinates with everyone
- [Your Mentor/Advisor] advises on quality across all functions
- [Your Ops Partner] handles operations and process

---

## Hiring Standards (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Every hire completes a qualifying task** reviewed by [Your Mentor/Advisor]/[Your Ops Partner] before starting paid work. No exceptions, no "he's a friend" shortcuts. We learned this lesson.
2. **Every hire starts with a 60-day probation period** with explicit quality benchmarks.
3. **Prefer Apprentice graduates.** They know the system, they've been trained, and we've seen their work.
4. **Document compensation clearly** before work begins. No ambiguity on pay structure.

---

## What This Unlocks

At 10 people and 10 Sprints/month:
- **$60k/month recurring revenue** from Sprints alone
- **[Your Name]'s time:** 60% sales, 20% strategic partnerships, 20% team leadership
- **Zero single points of failure** in delivery — every function has backup
- **Apprentice pipeline** creates a self-replenishing talent pool
- **Path to $250k Year 1** becomes conservative, not aspirational
