## Article Quality Standards

See [[blitzmetrics-canon/03-article-guidelines|Article Quality Guidelines]] for full quality gate with severity tiers. Key standards:

**BLOCK (zero tolerance):**
- No banned words (22 words — see canon 03)
- No banned AI patterns (13 patterns — see canon 03)
- No banned salesy language
- E-E-A-T compliant (real stories, real photos, real expertise — not fabricated)
- Minimum 200 words, sourced from real transcript/video
- Source video watched by a human before writing

**WARN (fix or document):**
- SEO-friendly titles leading with main keyword
- Proper heading hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)
- 2+ internal links with descriptive anchor text
- Featured image + inline images with alt text
- Source video embedded
- Categorized by Topic Wheel
- Meta description written (not auto-generated)
- No text walls (8+ line paragraphs)
- No stacked rhetorical questions (2+ in a row)
- No em dashes (unless voice profile permits)
- Contractions used naturally (flag if zero in 500+ words)

**STYLE (author judgment — not scored):**
- Paragraph rhythm should vary (one-liners for punch, longer for complexity)
- Single rhetorical questions are a legitimate technique
- Passive voice is fine when the subject isn't the point
- Active voice is the default, not an absolute
- Contraction density matches the speaker's natural voice

## Video Quality Standards

From the Master Video Editing Process Guide:

### One-Minute Video Checklist
- Square or portrait format (1080×1350 or 1080×1920)
- Hook in first 3 seconds (NO intro bumper)
- Lower third with name (5 seconds, beginning and end)
- Matching music (non-copyrighted)
- Captions: centered, readable font (white), max 3 lines, bottom placement
- No filler words ("uh," "um," "hm")
- No curse words (mute or beep)
- Remove low-authority moments (stuttering, dead air, nervousness)
- No "This is my first time speaking" or "I'm nervous" moments
- Audio synced with video
- Don't cut off head when filming
- Studio sound enabled in Descript

### WHY Video Specific
- Do NOT start sentences with "so"
- No intro bumper
- No copyrighted music or images
- No Google-searched images

### Speaker Reel
- Sizzle reel format
- Show audience engagement
- Multiple speaking clips
- Professional transitions

## Checklist Standards (CCS Framework)

Every checklist must have three sections:
1. **Qualifying Checklist:** Prerequisites — "Each entry must contain a word or a few words"
2. **Execution Checklist:** Steps — "Each entry must be an imperative sentence"
3. **Verification Checklist:** Validation — "Each entry must be a declarative sentence"

Each checklist item must be:
- S.M.A.R.T. (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-oriented)
- Include IF/THEN logic where possible
- Have a short video AND text explanation
- Proven by at least 3 people executing successfully 3 times each
