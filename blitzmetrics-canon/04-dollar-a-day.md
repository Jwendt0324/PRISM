# Dollar-a-Day Strategy — BlitzMetrics Canon

**Source:** Dollar-a-Day Webinar Deck, Content Factory Guides, Dennis Yu training
**Version:** 1.0 | **Extracted:** 2026-03-19

---

## Core Principle

Dollar-a-Day is NOT about spending $1/day forever. It's a systematic testing methodology that starts small to find winners, then scales what works. Only boost content that has PROVEN organic engagement first.

"We find the winners and boost them to gain the most leverage from the winning content."

---

## Prerequisites (Before Any Ad Spend)

1. Organic content must exist and be performing (Content Factory Stages 1-4 complete)
2. Minimum organic thresholds: 1,000 views OR 100+ engagements on video
3. Facebook Pixel / tracking installed and firing (Plumbing complete)
4. Custom audiences built (email lists, website visitors, video viewers)
5. Content Library tracking all assets with performance data
6. GCT defined for the campaign

---

## Phase 1: Testing ($1-5/day, 7 days)

**Setup:**
- Select 3-5 organic winners from Content Library
- Create dark posts (unpublished posts) for each
- Target initial audiences: custom audiences, 1% lookalikes, direct interests
- Set budget: $1-5/day per ad
- UTM parameters on all URLs

**Daily decision rules:**
- **Kill:** CTR < 1%, CPC > $2, relevance score < 5, negative feedback
- **Keep:** CTR > 2%, positive engagement, relevant comments
- **Test:** Try new audiences, new creative variations

**Quality gate:** 7-day window is STRICT. Document every kill/keep/test decision.

---

## Phase 2: Scaling ($5-20/day, 30 days)

**Actions:**
- Consolidate winning ads (typically 1-2 out of 5 tested)
- Scale budget gradually (not more than 20% increase per day)
- Expand audiences: broader lookalikes (1-3%), interest stacking
- Set up conversion tracking and measure CPA vs. CLV
- Daily optimization: adjust bids, rotate creatives, manage fatigue

**Quality gate:** CPA tracked and documented. End-of-phase decision documented. If CPA > CLV, pause and reassess creative/audience.

---

## Phase 3: Evergreen "Greatest Hits" ($20-50/day, ongoing)

**Actions:**
- Build rotation of 5-10 proven winners
- Monitor ad fatigue (frequency > 3 = rotate out)
- Monthly reporting: spend, CPA trend, sentiment, ROAS
- Quarterly audience refresh
- Bottom 20% retired, replaced with new Phase 1 graduates

**Quality gate:** Monthly review required. Fatigue assessed. No ad runs indefinitely without performance check.

---

## Campaign Health Dashboard (Monthly)

- Total spend by phase
- CPA trend (improving, stable, degrading)
- Top 3 performing ads with metrics
- Audience performance comparison
- Sentiment analysis (positive/negative comments)
- ROAS calculation
- Recommendations for next month

---

## Common Mistakes

1. Boosting content that has no organic traction (putting money behind zero)
2. Skipping Phase 1 testing and going straight to scale
3. Not tracking CPA against CLV
4. Running ads without proper Plumbing (no pixel, no tracking)
5. Not refreshing creatives (ad fatigue)
6. Using Dollar-a-Day for brand new products with no existing funnel
