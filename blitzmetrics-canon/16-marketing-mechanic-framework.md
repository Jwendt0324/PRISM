## The 7 Core Pillars

The first 7 episodes form the foundation — watch in order. Each builds on the last.

### Pillar 1: Entities
**Key point:** Google ranks entities, not websites. Your business must exist as a recognized entity before anything else works.
- Entity = person, place, or company in Google's database
- Must be connected to both industry and city
- Creates the geo-vertical grid (Pillar 2)

### Pillar 2: Geo-Vertical Grid
**Key point:** Rankings come from relationships, not keywords. Connect to industry AND geography.
- VERTICAL: Link to industry entities (software partners, suppliers, conferences)
- GEO: Link to local entities (community events, local businesses)
- Strengthens rankings because Google understands your place in the network

### Pillar 3: Content Strategy
**Key point:** Content is proof of expertise. Repurpose real stories across platforms.
- Feeds the entity with proof
- Forms the Topic Wheel (WHY/HOW/WHAT)
- Authentic, experience-driven content demonstrates E-E-A-T

### Pillar 4: MAA Optimization
**Key point:** Stop guessing. Use Metrics, Analysis, Action to find what works and double down.
- Identify which content/ads drive leads
- Double down on winners
- Turn high-performing content into ads and repurpose across platforms

### Pillar 5: Personal Branding
**Key point:** People buy from people they trust. Personal brand = fastest path to local authority.
- Not about fame or luxury cars — about connection
- Customer wants to know the person behind the company
- Stronger personal brand → better technicians, loyal customers, scalable opportunities

### Pillar 6: Website Architecture
**Key point:** Website is the central hub connecting company brand, personal brand, and all content.
- Must clearly display services, service areas, reviews, team stories, community involvement
- Optimized for Google, social, and AI discovery

### Pillar 7: Platform & Certification
**Key point:** The ecosystem that ties it all together.
- LSS platform, AI Apprentice program, certification
