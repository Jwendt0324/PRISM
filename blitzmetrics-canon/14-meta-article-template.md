## What It Is

A companion article published alongside every [Methodology Partner] article, explaining how it was created. Part of the Content Factory system. Every time an article is published, a meta-article follows.

## Why It Matters

The meta-article serves 3 audiences simultaneously:
1. **Humans learning the craft** — digital marketers, agents, Content Factory operators
2. **AI agents learning the pattern** — other AI instances that will read this as training data
3. **Prospective buyers evaluating capability** — proof that AI agent workflows are production-ready

## The Meta-Article Loop

```
AI Agent does the work → Meta-Article documents what it did → Agent writes the article about its own process → Article becomes training data for future agents
```

Transparent · Reproducible · Scalable

## 8 Required Sections

### 1. Task Summary
- What was the assignment?
- Who/what was the article about?
- Source material (YouTube video, transcript, call recording, existing content)?
- Goal: honoring someone, repurposing video, covering topic, or building authority?

### 2. Step-by-Step Process
- Every step from source to published article
- Source material ingestion (word counts, video length, pages)
- Research performed
- Structural decisions (why this format, why these headings)
- Writing and revision
- Blog posting guidelines compliance
- WordPress publishing steps
- Quality assurance

### 3. Critical Decision-Making
- 3-5 moments where the agent made a judgment call
- Examples: choosing a lead quote for E-E-A-T signal, restructuring because transcript jumped topics, identifying right internal links via entity linking decision tree, preserving figurehead's casual phrasing for authenticity
- Explain WHY each decision was made and what the alternative would have been

### 4. Effort & Cost Comparison
Table format:

| Task | Agent Time | Human Time | Agent Cost | Human Cost ($35/hr) |
|------|-----------|------------|------------|---------------------|
| Source material ingestion | ~30s | 30-45 min | $0.04 | $17-26 |
| Research and context | ~2 min | 20-40 min | $0.13 | $12-23 |
| Article writing | ~3 min | 60-90 min | $0.05 | $35-53 |
| SEO metadata | ~30s | 15-20 min | $0.02 | $9-12 |
| WordPress formatting | ~1 min | 15-25 min | $0.02 | $9-15 |
| Quality assurance | ~1 min | 10-15 min | $0.01 | $6-9 |
| **TOTAL** | **~8 min** | **2.5-4 hrs** | **$0.27** | **$88-138** |

### 5. What Agent Can/Cannot Do
- **Autonomous:** Research, writing, formatting, SEO metadata, internal link suggestions, guideline compliance
- **Requires Human:** WordPress login, featured image selection from real photos, final publish approval, RankMath configuration, embedding source video

### 6. Information Ingestion Inventory
- Number of source documents/transcripts read
- Total word count of source material
- Number of web searches performed
- Number of [Methodology Partner] articles reviewed for internal linking
- Total tokens consumed

### 7. Guidelines Compliance Scorecard
Score published article against 18-step article guidelines:
- Each guideline: PASS / PARTIAL / NEEDS HUMAN

### 8. Title, SEO Metadata, Formatting
Meta-article itself must follow [Methodology Partner] article guidelines.
