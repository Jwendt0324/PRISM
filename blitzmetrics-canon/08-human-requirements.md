## Purpose

## Content Production (Stage 1: Produce)

| Task | Why Human Required |
|------|--------------------|
| Filming video content | Only the business owner can tell their stories |
| Photography at events/work | Must be physically present, geotagged |
| Client interviews | Relationship and rapport required |
| WHY video recording | Personal mission can't be delegated |
| Podcast hosting/guesting | Real-time conversation skills |
| Speaking at events | Physical presence, audience engagement |

**Rule:** The business owner must produce the raw content. This is the 1% they must do. Everything else can be delegated.

## Content Quality (Stage 2: Process)

| Task | Why Human Required |
|------|--------------------|
| Watching source video before writing | Context, tone, intent not captured in transcript alone |
| Name/brand spelling verification | AI can't verify against real-world spelling |
| Voice and tone approval | "Would [Your Name] actually say this?" |
| E-E-A-T compliance review | Ensuring real stories, not fabricated ones |
| AI language detection | Subtle AI patterns require trained eye |
| Figurehead context | Understanding relationships between people mentioned |

**Rule:** [Your Mentor/Advisor] flagged AI language in [Your Content Specialist]'s initial work. Every AI-assisted piece must be reviewed by a human who knows the client's voice.

## Publishing (Stage 3: Post)

| Task | Why Human Required |
|------|--------------------|

| Category/tag selection | Must align with client's Topic Wheel strategy |
| Featured image selection | Must be real, relevant photo (not stock) |
