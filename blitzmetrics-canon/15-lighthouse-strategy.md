## What It Is

The most powerful way for an agency to dominate any niche: partner with a trusted authority (the "lighthouse") and let their credibility become yours. Their reputation becomes your reputation. Their audience becomes your audience.

## How It Works

1. **Identify** the most respected/visible person in your target vertical
2. **Partner** with them — build services/agency under their name
3. **Compensate** them (~5% royalty on revenue generated)
4. **Leverage** their audience, credibility, and social proof
5. **Publish** results and bring the lighthouse on stage to present

## Inception Model

**Source:** `auth-02.txt` ([Your Mentor/Advisor], authority building through connections)

The Inception Model is one level deeper than direct marketing: **"Influence the people your target audience respects."**

Instead of targeting your buyer directly, target the influencers your buyer follows. When those influencers talk about you (or your lighthouse), their audience receives a trusted referral -- not an ad.

**How it connects to Lighthouse:**
- The lighthouse IS the inception target. By partnering with a vertical authority, you gain automatic inception into their audience.
- Every interview, co-presentation, or co-authored piece with the lighthouse creates inception moments.
- Dollar-a-Day amplifies inception content to ensure it reaches the influencer's full audience, not just organic reach.

**Qasim YouTube Example:** Qasim posted 10+ minute videos daily for 90 days. After 90 days, YouTube started suggesting his content. 70% retention at 1 minute. The inception: his content featured industry leaders, so their audiences discovered him through association.
