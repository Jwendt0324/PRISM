# Thank You Machine — [Methodology Partner] Canon

**Source:** Master Content Factory Guide (CoachYu v9.9), Content Factory Guide v1.2, Dollar-a-Day Presentation v1.4
**Version:** 1.0 | **Extracted:** 2026-04-01

---

## Core Principle

"It's not what you say about yourself that matters but what others say about you."

The Thank You Machine is a systematic 6-step process for generating influence through gratitude at scale. It sits alongside Dollar-a-Day as one of two engines in the Promote stage of the Content Factory. While Dollar-a-Day amplifies content through paid ads, the Thank You Machine generates organic social proof through personalized gestures of gratitude.

Influence = Currency. The Thank You Machine creates a virtuous cycle: gratitude generates endorsements, endorsements generate content, content gets amplified, amplification generates more relationships.

---

## The 6-Step Client Love Process

### Step 1: Notification of Positive Mention
- Monitor for positive mentions using mention.com or manual social listening
- Track all mentions in the **Positive Mention Bank** (see below)
- Alert team within 24 hours of any positive mention

### Step 2: Ask for Lightweight Permission
- Contact the person who mentioned you
- Ask: "Can I quote you on that?"
- This is a LOW-FRICTION ask -- most people say yes
- Record permission status in the Positive Mention Bank (Y/N)

### Step 3: Add Endorsement to Company Materials
- Add the quote to website testimonials page
- Include in relevant service pages or case studies
- Add to Content Library for future repurposing
- Tag with Topic Wheel position

### Step 4: Client Love (Say Thank You)
- Send a personalized gesture from the gift tier system (see below)
- The gesture should be unexpected and personal
- Recipients share gifts on social media -- this IS the content generation mechanism

### Step 5: Create Follow-On Content
- Turn endorsements into derivative content:
  - Quote cards with the person's photo and quote
  - Listicles ("10 Things Our Clients Say About...")
  - Video compilations of testimonial clips
  - Social posts tagging the endorser

### Step 6: Amplify Endorsement via Dollar-a-Day
- Boost the best endorsement content through [[blitzmetrics-canon/04-dollar-a-day|Dollar-a-Day]]
- Target the endorser's network and lookalike audiences
- This creates maximum leverage: authentic endorsement + paid reach

## Positive Mention Bank

A structured Google Sheet that serves as the central tracking system for all endorsements and mentions.

### Required Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Source Name | Person who made the mention |
| Source Company | Their organization |
| Quote | Exact words used |
| Date | When the mention occurred |
| Permission to Use | Y/N |
| URL | Link to original mention |
| Link to Image | Drive folder with screenshots |
| Network/Media Out | Where it was posted |
| Notes | Follow-up actions, gift sent, etc. |

### Collection Process

1. Set up mention.com alerts for brand name, founder name, key product names
2. When mention detected, screenshot and log immediately
3. Ask permission within 48 hours
4. Add approved mentions to Content Library
5. Create derivative content (quote cards, listicles)
6. Amplify via Dollar-a-Day

### Testimonial Collection at Events

1. Find a quiet place with good lighting
2. Film horizontally (landscape orientation)
3. Lead with: "What was your biggest takeaway?"
4. Keep under 1 minute
5. Upload to Dropbox/YouTube same day

### Tied to Payment Amount (Keap/Infusionsoft)

| < $500 | Custom socks |
| < $1,000 | Custom socks + branded shirt |

## The Flywheel Effect

The Thank You Machine creates a self-reinforcing loop:

1. Content Factory produces content (Produce > Process > Post)
2. Promote stage has TWO engines: Thank You Machine + Dollar-a-Day Ads
3. Thank You Machine generates organic social proof (recipient shares gift on social media)
4. Dollar-a-Day amplifies both the original content AND the organic social proof
5. Amplified endorsements attract more relationships
6. More relationships generate more endorsements
7. Cycle repeats with increasing momentum

## Connection to Client Retention (Stoplight Method)

The Thank You Machine is a proactive retention tool. Combined with the Stoplight Method for monitoring client happiness:

- **Green clients** receive Thank You Machine gestures as relationship deepening
- **Yellow clients** receive accelerated Thank You Machine attention to re-engage
- **Red clients** may need direct intervention beyond the Thank You Machine

Regular Thank You Machine execution keeps more clients in Green status. Retention is cheaper than acquisition.

## Human Requirements

1. **Permission requests must come from a human** -- automated "Can I quote you?" messages feel transactional
2. **Gift selection requires human judgment** -- personalization is the entire point; generic gifts defeat the purpose
3. **Event testimonial filming requires a human** -- someone must physically be at the event
4. **Endorsement quality review** -- humans must verify quotes are appropriate before amplification
5. **Relationship context** -- only humans know the relationship history that determines the right gesture
